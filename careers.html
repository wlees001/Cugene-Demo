Join the Team

Cugene’s laboratories are located in Waltham, Massachusetts, less than 5 miles from Cambridge, the world’s premier biotechnology hub. It is within walking distance of the Waverley station of the Boston commuter rail, providing a convenient connection to Boston’s North Station and all of New England.
Cugene offers its employees a competitive and wide-ranging package of benefits. This includes paid vacation time and holidays, traditional and Roth 401k retirement plans (with company match), and medical and dental insurance options. As members of MassBio, Cugene employees have access to a variety of discounts for museums, restaurants, and other local events.
